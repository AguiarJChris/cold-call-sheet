▶"I'll wait until rates come down to buy" new
"Everyone is waiting for rates to drop — which means the second they do, every buyer floods the market and you're competing against everyone. ▶"I can't find anything to buy" new
"That's the most common concern I hear right now — and it's something we can solve. "That's great — and I'd never tell you to ignore it. But before you accept: investors make money by buying below market. My job is to get you above market. Let me show you what the open market would bring — if the investor's offer wins, I'll tell you. Does 5 work or is 6 better?"
▶"My neighbor's house has been sitting forever" new
"Do you know why that home is sitting? Nine times out of ten it's a pricing or marketing problem, not a market problem. My team has sold homes in this neighborhood consistently. The right strategy makes all the difference. Can I show you what we'd do differently?"
▶"Zillow says my home is worth more" new
